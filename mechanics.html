Game Mechanics

The game is played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking a space with an "X" or an "O". The first player to align three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.

If all 9 spaces are filled and neither player has three in a row, the game is a tie.

In Endless Mode, when the board fills up without a winner, the oldest move is removed to free a space. This allows players to continue playing indefinitely until someone wins.
